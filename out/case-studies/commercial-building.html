Commercial Building Electrical Installation

Client: Heritage Heights Developers

Real Estate

Project Overview

This project demonstrates our capability to supply high-quality electrical components for real estate applications. We provided comprehensive product solutions that met all technical requirements and project deadlines.

Request Similar Quote

The Challenge

A new 20-story commercial building required complete electrical infrastructure including wiring, circuit protection, and power distribution for offices, retail spaces, and common areas.

Our Solution

We supplied PVC conduits, premium wiring devices (switches and sockets), circuit breakers, cable lugs, earthing rods, tinned copper busbars for distribution boards, and inspection chambers for underground cable access. All products were delivered on schedule to meet construction deadlines.
